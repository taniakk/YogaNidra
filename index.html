🌸 Yoga Philosophy

What Is Yoga Nidra?

Patanjali Muni defines Yoga as "Chitta Vritti Nirodh", the gentle practice of allowing the mind to settle rather than forcing it into silence. Yoga Nidra develops this effortless awareness through conscious relaxation, creating harmony between body, mind, and breath.

🧘

Yoga + Nidra

Union with conscious sleep. A deeply restorative state where the body rests completely while the mind remains gently aware, observing without judgment.

🌿

Master Sadhana

A few minutes of Yoga Nidra may provide relaxation comparable to hours of restful sleep while strengthening positive intentions (Sankalpa).

💜

Adhi & Vyadhi

According to Yoga Vasistha, the root of disease begins in the mind. Yoga Nidra gently addresses this origin through conscious relaxation, helping restore emotional balance and inner harmony.

Inner Stillness

"Yoga Nidra is the art of conscious relaxation where healing begins from within."

🤰 Prenatal Wellness

Yoga Nidra for Expecting Mothers

A gentle practice that nurtures emotional balance, inner peace, and healthy prenatal development for both mother and baby.

🌱

Sowing Sanskara Seeds

"Shuddha beejapoti fale rasal gomati" — Fruits with pure seeds are naturally healthy and nourishing.

During pregnancy, Yoga Nidra creates favourable conditions for healthy intrauterine growth and development.

Stress releases hormones into the mother's bloodstream, which may influence the baby's well-being. Yoga Nidra helps reduce this stress, promoting calmness, emotional stability, and a healthier pregnancy journey.

💜

Why It Matters

Building emotional resilience during pregnancy.

This research integrates multiple branches of Yoga to create a holistic approach for prenatal wellness. The intention is to bridge existing research gaps by encouraging Yathashakti (according to one's capacity) and Nirantar (consistent practice).

Yoga Nidra has shown positive effects across diverse groups of pregnant women, irrespective of demographic background, making it an inclusive and accessible relaxation practice.

🌸 Yogic Philosophy

Yoga Nidra & Five Koshas

According to Yogic philosophy, Yoga Nidra gently guides awareness through the five layers (Koshas) of human existence, promoting complete physical, mental, emotional and spiritual wellbeing.

🌿

Annamaya Kosha

Physical Body

Yoga Nidra relaxes muscles, reduces fatigue, improves body awareness, and promotes deep physical restoration.

💨

Pranamaya Kosha

Energy Body

Regulates breath, balances life-force energy, and supports healthy physiological functions during pregnancy.

🧠

Manomaya Kosha

Mental Body

Reduces anxiety, calms emotional fluctuations, and cultivates inner peace through mindful awareness.

✨

Vijnanamaya Kosha

Wisdom Body

Develops self-awareness, intuition, and conscious decision-making through deeper relaxation.

🪷

Anandamaya Kosha

Bliss Body

Encourages profound relaxation, meditation, happiness, and a lasting sense of inner harmony.

Holistic Transformation

Yoga Nidra integrates all five Koshas, helping expecting mothers experience complete wellbeing by harmonizing the body, breath, mind, wisdom, and inner bliss.
