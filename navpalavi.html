What You Will Experience

A complete, holistic programme for the mind, body, and soul of mother, father, and baby.

🧘

योग (Yoga)

Safe, trimester-specific asana sequences for strength, flexibility, and birth preparation. Breath-body connection practices for labour readiness.

🌿

आयुर्वेद (Ayurveda)

Garbhini Paricharya — ancient Ayurvedic protocols for each trimester. Herbal nutrition, lifestyle adjustments, and Ayurvedic massage techniques.

🥗

पोषण (Nutrition)

Evidence-based prenatal nutrition guidance. Sattvic diet principles combined with modern nutritional science for optimal foetal development.

🤲

मुद्रा (Mudra)

Therapeutic hand gestures to balance energy channels. Yoni Mudra, Apana Mudra, and Prithvi Mudra for prenatal health and ease of delivery.

💬

समुपदेशन (Counselling)

Individual and group counselling sessions addressing fears, anxieties, and emotional challenges of pregnancy. Building confidence and inner strength.

👫

Partner Counselling

Involving partners in the journey. Sessions for fathers-to-be on emotional support, bonding, and building a conscious, nurturing family environment.

🧠

Multiple Intelligence Dev.

Stimulating all eight intelligences in the unborn child through specific music, stories, movements, and mindful interaction with the baby in the womb.

🌟

Positive Affirmations

Daily affirmation practices to reprogram subconscious beliefs about pregnancy, birth, and motherhood. Building deep inner confidence and positivity.

🎵

Music & Garbha Sangeet

Classical ragas, soothing mantras, and Garbha Sangeet for foetal brain development. Music as medicine — for mother's calm and baby's bliss.
