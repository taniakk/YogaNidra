🌸 Together We Offer

A unique holistic programme that combines the therapeutic benefits of Yoga, Yoga Nidra, Pranayama and Ayurvedic prenatal care, supporting women physically, mentally, emotionally and spiritually throughout pregnancy and beyond.

What You Will Experience

A complete, holistic programme for the mind, body, and soul of mother, father, and baby.

🧘

योग (Yoga)

Safe, trimester-specific asana sequences for strength, flexibility, and birth preparation. Breath-body connection practices for labour readiness.

🌿

आयुर्वेद (Ayurveda)

Garbhini Paricharya, herbal nutrition, lifestyle adjustments and Ayurvedic massage techniques for every trimester.

🥗

पोषण (Nutrition)

Evidence-based prenatal nutrition guidance combining Sattvic diet principles with modern nutritional science.

🤲

मुद्रा (Mudra)

Therapeutic hand gestures such as Yoni Mudra, Apana Mudra and Prithvi Mudra for prenatal health.

💬

समुपदेशन (Counselling)

Individual and group counselling sessions to reduce anxiety, build confidence and emotional wellbeing.

👫

Partner Counselling

Sessions helping fathers-to-be provide emotional support, bonding and create a nurturing family.

🧠

Multiple Intelligence

Activities using music, stories and mindful interaction to stimulate the baby's overall development.

🌟

Positive Affirmations

Daily affirmations that create confidence, positivity and emotional resilience throughout pregnancy.

🎵

Music & Garbha Sangeet

Classical ragas, soothing mantras and Garbha Sangeet promoting peace for mother and baby.
